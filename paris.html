Paris

Paris, the capital of France, is a historic and vibrant metropolis with over 2 million residents. Nestled along the Seine River, its rich cultural heritage and iconic landmarks make it a global center for art, fashion, and romance.

Founded by the Celts and later named Lutetia by the Romans, Paris has been a key player in shaping European history and continues to captivate visitors with its timeless charm.
